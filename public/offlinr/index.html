Flickr Offlinr

What is Flickr Offlinr

Flickr Offlinr is a Windows Store application to view photos and videos on Flickr in offline.

Flickr Offlinr app for Windows in the Windows Store

Privacy Policy

Flickr Offlinr does not collect any personal information. After signing in to Flickr from the app it only stores an authentication token and does not store your Flickr user name and password.
